Ang Pangarap Kong Holdap

Set in the imaginary Barangay Husay, Eman teams up with his two friends, Toto and Carlo who start as the underdogs, failing at every opportunity to succeed in their small-time scams. They then meet Nicoy who becomes part of Eman's group breaking the tradition of having only 3 members per gang. Basically it is a movie about a group of useless thieves who are doing many different techniques in order to steal from someone but they they always somehow manage to fail. This is one of my most favorite movies because it made me laugh alot and that it isn't all about stealing, it is also about how parents still love their children even though the children always makes different kinds of mistakes.

A Silent Voice

A silent Voice is a 2016 Japanese animated drama film produced by Kyoto Animation. It is directed by Naoko Yamada and written by Reiko Yoshida, featuring character designs by Futoshi Nishiya and music by kensuke Ushio. It is a movie about a girl who was bullied throughout her Elementary years and suddenly changed school, the bully was also bullied because of what he did to the girl. It is a film about bullying and the effects of bullying to the persons mind. It can cause suicidal thoughts and can eventually lead the other person through depression or suicide. It is one of my favorite because of this movie taught me that anything I do or say can affect someone deeply and I won't even realize it.

VHS

It is one of the most gruesome movies I have watched til this day, the film features different types of plot, it doesn't feature a lot of jumpscares but there are alot of gruesome scenes. I would definitely watch it again.
